My Childhood

Growing up in Eldoret, my childhood was a mix of fun, challenges, and adventure. The experiences I had shaped my character, values, and future aspirations.

Early Life

From an early age, I was exposed to the world of coffee farming. This not only connected me to nature but also taught me the importance of hard work and patience. Eldoret, with its vibrant environment, gave me the freedom to explore and learn from different experiences.

Key Moments

One of the most defining aspects of my childhood was being involved in coffee farming. Observing the entire process—from planting to harvesting—taught me the value of persistence and dedication. This hands-on experience nurtured my appreciation for agriculture and business.

Lessons Learned

My childhood instilled in me the values of resilience, teamwork, and problem-solving. The combination of challenges and adventure helped me develop a mindset that embraces both discipline and creativity. These lessons continue to shape my journey in software engineering and cybersecurity.
